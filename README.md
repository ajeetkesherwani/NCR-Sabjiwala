# NCR-Sabjiwala 🥦🥬

NCR Sabjiwala is an online vegetable delivery platform dedicated to providing fresh, farm-sourced produce to homes across the National Capital Region (NCR). With a focus on quality, convenience, and supporting local farmers, we bring daily essentials to your doorstep — fast and hassle-free.

🛒 Features

🥕 Wide Selection of Fresh Vegetables

📦 Same-Day & Scheduled Delivery

📍 Service Across Delhi, Noida, Gurugram, Ghaziabad & Faridabad

💳 Secure Online Payments & Cash on Delivery

🧾 Easy Order Management and History

🎯 Our Mission

To simplify access to fresh, affordable, and hygienic vegetables while empowering local farmers and vendors through digital commerce.

🛠️ Tech Stack

Frontend: React / Flutter

Backend: Node.js / Express

Database: MongoDB / PostgreSQL

Payments: Razorpay / UPI

Admin Dashboard & Vendor Portal
